About JP Consulting

JP Consulting Group Ltd is a New Zealand-based consulting company supporting international business development, education-sector advisory, and cross-border partnerships.

Built on a foundation of international education, institutional management, and business networks, JP Consulting provides practical advisory support for organisations seeking to enter, test, and grow in the New Zealand and Asia-Pacific markets.

Our work focuses on feasibility review, buyer development, business matching, localisation strategy, and project execution for companies looking for sustainable market expansion.
